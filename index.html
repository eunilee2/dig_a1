Dataset: Fatal Accidental Overdoses in Allegheny County 2007 - 2025

This dataset contains 7,478 entries of fatal accidental overdose cases in the Allegheny County region of Pennsylvania. It only includes cases from 2007 to 2025 that have been closed, meaning that a final conclusion about the case is made after following all steps of the procedure in administering the case investigation. Each case contains three types of information: 1. the geotemporal data of each case that covers the death date and time, case year, and incident zip, 2. demographic data that includes the case subject's age, sex, and race, and 3. the polysubstance data that covers the types of drugs used and the number of drugs used in each overdose case. One thing to note about this data is that incident zip indicates the zip code of where the Office of the Medical Examiner received the body, not necessarily where the overdose occurred. Nevertheless, as we explore the dataset, I hope to uncover more detail in understanding emerging temporal trends in drug cases and quantitative trends in the polysubstance nature of cases. Understanding this can allow us to compare overdose deaths on a year-by-year basis and potentially how current policies and interventions might play a role in these results.

This data can be found online at: https://data.wprdc.org/dataset/allegheny-county-fatal-accidental-overdoses/resource/1c59b26a-1684-4bfb-92f7-205b947530cf
